Some Chinese Tutoring School For Thai Learner

Zhongxin สถาบันสอนภาษาจีนจงซิน

Zhongxin Chinese Language Institute uses the international standard curriculum from Beijing Language and Culture University (BLCU). The courses are suitable for both beginners and advanced learners, and are taught by qualified Chinese and Thai teachers with real teaching experience.
Students can join online Chinese courses at any level, from basic to advanced, with flexible scheduling and the choice of private or group classes.

Available courses include: Basic Chinese, Children’s Chinese, HSK preparation, Business Chinese, and more.

Go to the tutoring school's website

OKLS

OKLS carefully selects and develops the most suitable textbooks for each course. Every course are designed to fit the learner’s age and learning goals, making study both practical and effective. All programs are officially accredited by the Ministry of Education.
OKLS has also received official recognition from the Ministry of Education, earning a “Very Good” rating in its internal quality assurance from the Office of the Private Education Commission.

Courses offered: Chinese for Children, General Chinese for Adults.

Go to the tutoring school's website

Globish Chinese

Globish offers online Chinese courses for learners of all ages, from beginners to advanced. The programs focus on practical speaking skills, helping students improve pronunciation, fluency, and confidence through real conversations with experienced Chinese teachers who understand the needs of Thai learners.

Courses offered: Chinese for daily life, Chinese for work, HSK preparation, and etc.

Go to the tutoring school's website

Open Durian

Online Chinese Courses that doesn't required any prior knowledge.
Programs range from basic Chinese to HSK3 preparation, covering all four skills: listening, speaking, reading, and writing. After finishing the course you will definitely be able to speak Chinese. Learners can choose from 3 different study plans depending on their preferences. A support team is always available to provide guidance on Chinese language issues.

Courses offered: Quick Chinese Course, Pinyin Course, HSK3 Made Easy with Suilin, Beginner Chinese Course, and more.

Go to the tutoring school's website

โรงเรียนสอนภาษาจีนเพียรอักษร (หงเหล่าซือสอนภาษาจีน)

All courses are led by Hong Laoshi, founder of Pien Aksorn Chinese Language School, offering affordable Chinese education with Ministry-recognized certificates. His online programs use practical dialogues, include versions with and without Pinyin, and provide exercises with answer keys.

ourses offered: Chinese for beginners, Standard Chinese, HSK preparation, and more.

Go to the tutoring school's website

Panda chinese

Panda Chinese is a specialized Chinese language school with native-speaking teachers who make learning more effective. Teachers can also communicate clearly in Thai, ensuring language is not a barrier. The school offers a variety of courses suitable for all ages and levels, allowing even beginners to become proficient in Chinese.

The courses include HSK-level video lessons focusing on grammar, one-on-one classes that emphasize listening and speaking skills, and a lot more.

Go to the tutoring school's website
